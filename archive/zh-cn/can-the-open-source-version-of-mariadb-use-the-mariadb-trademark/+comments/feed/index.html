By "open-source version" I presume you just mean MariaDB Server (sometimes referred to as MariaDB Community Server by MariaDB Corporation). There's also MariaDB Enterprise Server offered by MariaDB Corporation, and this is also open-source.</p> <p>"Use" can be ambiguous, but the MariaDB Foundation have exclusive rights to use "MariaDB Server" and the logo to further the MariaDB Foundation mission.
