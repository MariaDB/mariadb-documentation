Release Notes Discrepancy

I suspect there is a typo in one of these sections:

On this page:

https://mariadb.com/kb/en/release-notes/

You say 10.10 is the current short-term stable series and 10.6 is the current long-term stable series.

This section contains the release notes for MariaDB releases. This section is divided into the major MariaDB series. The current long-term stable series is MariaDB 10.6, the current short-term stable series is MariaDB 10.10, and the current development series is MariaDB 10.11.

On this page:

https://mariadb.com/kb/en/mariadb-server-release-dates/

You say 10.11 is the current long-term series and 10.6 is the previous long-term.

I suspect this second page is correct and it's a timing "thing."

Bill

Answer Answered by Daniel Black in this comment.

Thanks Bill.

You're right.

I've updated the first link. I just got a bit outdated.

I've updated our release checklist.
