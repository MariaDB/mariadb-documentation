MariaDB Knowledge Base Comments for: MariaDB Galera Cluster Questionshttps://mariadb.com/kb/en/mariadb-galera-cluster-questions/+comments/feed/en-usMon, 05 May 2025 12:33:20 +0000Re: MariaDB Galera Cluster Questionshttps://mariadb.com/kb/en/mariadb-galera-cluster-questions/+comments/968<p>Answered by Justin Swanhart on the <a href="https://lists.launchpad.net/maria-discuss/msg01243.html">mailing list</a>:</p> <div style="margin-left:2em"><p> Galera is a higher availability, not a sharding system. You can use spider or shard-query to handle transparent sharding. Spider is a storage engine and does most work in a single thread. Shard query is MPP and executes sort, join and group operations in parallel, but joins between shards are not possible unless you use sub queries in the from clause.</p> </div> Sergei Golubchikhttps://mariadb.com/kb/en/mariadb-galera-cluster-questions/+comments/968
