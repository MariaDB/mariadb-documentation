MariaDB Knowledge Base Comments for: Licensing in non embedded solutionhttps://mariadb.com/kb/en/licensing-in-non-embedded-solution/+comments/feed/en-usMon, 09 Dec 2024 15:57:27 +0000Re: Licensing in non embedded solutionhttps://mariadb.com/kb/en/licensing-in-non-embedded-solution/+comments/115<p>In case 1, you are not affected by the GPL because:</p> <ul start="1"><li>You are not linking with GPL software. </li><li>Your application doesn't require MariaDB to work. </li></ul> <p>The fact that the user can use a GPL program like MariaDB with your application doesn't in any way put any restrictions or license requirements on your application.</p> <p>In case 2, you are not distributing any GPL code and thus you are not affected by the GPL in any way.</p> <p>Note that in many cases like this you would be probably be fine even if you would distribute the MySQL/MariaDB connectors and MariaDB with your application.
