MariaDB Knowledge Base Comments for: Maria DB - changing hosting with a different DBhttps://mariadb.com/kb/en/maria-db-changing-hosting-with-a-different-db/+comments/feed/en-usMon, 30 Dec 2024 12:31:40 +0000Re: Maria DB - changing hosting with a different DBhttps://mariadb.com/kb/en/maria-db-changing-hosting-with-a-different-db/+comments/1059<p>You are either moving from MariaDB to MySQL, or from Aria to MyISAM. Aria was formerly called Maria, which probably still confuses some people. Your example makes me think that the second hypotesys is correct.</p> <p>If you are moving to MyISAM, you should change ENGINE=Aria to ENGINE=MyISAM, and remove PAGE_CHECKSUM, because it is an Aria-specific clause. You can obtain something similar with CHECKSUM=1.</p> <p>For the exact meaning of these clauses, see <a href="https://mariadb.com/kb/en/create-table/">https://mariadb.com/kb/en/create-table/</a></p> Federico Razzolihttps://mariadb.com/kb/en/maria-db-changing-hosting-with-a-different-db/+comments/1059
