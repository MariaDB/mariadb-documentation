'.bak'); print &#34;Made it to rename\n&#34;; open(IN, '&lt;' . $file . '.bak') or die $!; print &#34;Made it to open input file\n&#34;; open(OUT, '&gt;' . $file) or die $!; print &#34;Made it to open output file\n&#34;; while(&lt;IN&gt;) { $_ =~ s/Offset/`Offset`/g; print OUT $_; } close(IN); close(OUT); </pre><p>The substitution was successful and then I ran "cat tz.fix | mysql -u root -p mysql".
