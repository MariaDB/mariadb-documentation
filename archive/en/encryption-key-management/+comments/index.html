Re: Encryption Key Management

There is a small typo:

# openssl enc -aes-256-cbc -md sha11 -k your_passwd \
      -in /etc/mysql/keys -out /etc/mysql/keys.enc

Should be:

# openssl enc -aes-256-cbc -md sha1 -k your_passwd \
      -in /etc/mysql/keys -out /etc/mysql/keys.enc

sha11 doesn't exist.
