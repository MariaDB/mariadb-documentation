7 years, 10 months ago Ian Gilfillan
Re: Questions vs. comments

It is confusing. The reason is that the mechanism for categories and for pages is different. Questions appear as a page on a category, pages themselves have comments. It's been suggested before that the two concepts be merged, but with the legacy differences it's not a trivial undertaking.
