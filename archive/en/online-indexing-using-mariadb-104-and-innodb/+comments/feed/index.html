MariaDB Knowledge Base Comments for: online indexing using mariadb 10.4 and innodb?https://mariadb.com/kb/en/online-indexing-using-mariadb-104-and-innodb/+comments/feed/en-usMon, 03 Feb 2025 14:00:59 +0000Re: online indexing using mariadb 10.4 and innodb?https://mariadb.com/kb/en/online-indexing-using-mariadb-104-and-innodb/+comments/4232<p>At the start and end of an <code>ALTER TABLE</code> or <code>CREATE INDEX</code> or <code>DROP INDEX</code> or similar operation, the table will be locked exclusively, that is, any active transactions that may have accessed the table must be committed or aborted for the operation to continue. It is called ‘online’ <code>ALTER TABLE</code> because during time-consuming operations (creating secondary indexes, or rebuilding the table and all its indexes), concurrent modifications are allowed. <a href="https://mariadb.com/kb/en/library/innodb-online-ddl-overview/#alter-locking-strategies">https://mariadb.com/kb/en/library/innodb-online-ddl-overview/#alter-locking-strategies</a> has been updated to make this clearer.</p> Marko Mäkelähttps://mariadb.com/kb/en/online-indexing-using-mariadb-104-and-innodb/+comments/4232
