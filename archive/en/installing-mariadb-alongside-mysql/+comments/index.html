6 years, 11 months ago M Taha Masood
Re: Installing MariaDB Alongside MySQL

solved it by setting the macro in /etc/init.d/mariadb at line 284 , as it is being evaluated to the one used by MySql instance.

mysqld_pid_file_path=$datadir/`hostname`.pid

Other than this the instructions on this webpage work like a charm ! thanks Taha

I had to add --defaults-file=/opt/mariadb-data/my.cnf to the line parse_server_arguments `$print_defaults --defaults-file=/opt/mariadb-data/my.cnf $extra_args mysqld server mysql_server mysql.server`
