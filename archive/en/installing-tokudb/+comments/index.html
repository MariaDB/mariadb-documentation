10 years, 6 months ago Sergei Golubchik
Re: How to enable TokuDB in MariaDB 10.0

There is storage/toludb *already* in 10.0 tree, you don't need to download TokuDB from github. In fact, TokuDB from github won't compile in MariaDB source tree.

You need gcc 4.7 or later and cmake 2.8.9 or later to build with TokuDB.
